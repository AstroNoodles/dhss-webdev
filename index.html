Max The Dog

  • Where did we get him?

    In the great land of Long Island in the town of Ronkonkoma. He was bred and we picked him up.

Miscellaneous Fun Facts

  • Max is a Chinese Crested and is one of the few types of hairless dogs that exist. There also exists a powderpuff version of the Chinese Crested and it usually wins dog grooming and fashion competitions.
  • Spanish explorers found the Chinese Crested in Mexico and parts of Central and South America as early as in the 1500s. Europeans found the breed in Africa in the 1700s.
  • He likes to hang out with his brother Ringo, an American orange tabby. He's been here since the owner was 7 years old!
  • If my mom would have her way, we would also have a Sphinx cat in the house so we wouldn't have to clean for fur so often. It is the only hairless cat breed that exists, along with the Donkskoy Cat.
